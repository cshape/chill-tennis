Chill Tennis

Tennis is a wonderful game to play with friends. But it's not much fun if you can't keep a rally going.

Most casual players jump too quickly into games and don't give themselves a chance to get a good foundation. This leads to frustration down the line, as you won't improve.

Work with Cale to improve your game in a fun, low-pressure ("chill") environment, and then wow your friends the next time you go play!

"80 percent of success is showing up"
